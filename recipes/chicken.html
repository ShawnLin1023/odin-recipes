Lasagna Recipes

These flavorful chicken bites are tossed in a garlic butter pan sauce with white wine, fresh lemon, and simple seasonings. Serve it as an easy dinner with mashed potatoes or an elevated appetizer with toothpicks!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 4 tablespoons salted butter
  • Fresh lemon slices

Steps

  1. Combine the seasonings for the chicken and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients before beginning.
  2. Cover the chicken with plastic wrap and gently tenderize each side with a meat mallet for more tender results.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken in batches, turning to sear on all sides, for about 5 minutes total, or until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add the chicken back to the skillet along with any juices from the plate.
  5. Squeeze 1-2 fresh lemon slices over the dish.
