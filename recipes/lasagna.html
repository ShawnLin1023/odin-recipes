Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 5 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 lb. ground Italian sausage

Steps

  1. Prep Work: Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside. Measure out remaining ingredients.
  2. Make the Sauce
  3. Preheat Oven and Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  4. Assemble: Spray foil with nonstick cooking spray and place it spray-side-down on top of the baking dish to prevent the cheese from sticking to it.
  5. Bake: Bake for 35 minutes. Remove cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
